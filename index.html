MEET SHANI

A bit about how it all began...

Hello, my name is Shani Madhu, a professional artist with over 20 years of experience creating a wide range of artworks. My artistic journey spans watercolour, colour pencil, acrylic, and oil paintings, with a strong foundation in both traditional and contemporary techniques. Over the years, I have been honoured to receive awards in various art competitions, recognising my dedication and passion for fine art.

I hold a Diploma in 2D Drawing and Animation, which has further enhanced my understanding of form, detail, and visual storytelling. For the past 5 years in the UK, I have been specialising in commissioned artworks, working closely with clients to create meaningful, hand-crafted pieces that capture emotion, personality, and lasting memories. Each artwork is carefully created with attention to detail, making every artwork unique.
